## 1. What Is ExiusCart? {#what-is-exiuscart}

ExiusCart is a next-generation, cloud-based business management platform designed to give small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) the same level of operational power that was previously only available to large enterprises. Think of it as your entire business brain — from ringing up a sale at your retail counter, to running payroll, managing supplier invoices, sending email marketing campaigns, scheduling customer appointments, tracking your fleet of vehicles, and monitoring every employee's attendance — all inside one unified dashboard.

Built with a modern technology stack (Next.js 15, FastAPI, and a PostgreSQL-backed REST API), ExiusCart is fast, mobile-responsive, and designed with the real-world needs of business owners in mind. Whether you operate a single boutique in Dubai, a chain of electronics shops across Sri Lanka, or a growing services company serving international clients, ExiusCart adapts to your currency, your language, your workflows, and your scale.

At its core, ExiusCart is structured around the same philosophy as leading enterprise platforms — but without the enterprise price tag or the six-month implementation nightmare. You sign up, connect your shop, and you are operational within hours.

ExiusCart is not just a POS system. It is not just an inventory tool. It is a full ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) suite that brings together:

- **Point of Sale (POS)** with receipt printing and WhatsApp sharing
- **Inventory management** with low stock alerts and bulk CSV import
- **Financial accounting** with multi-currency VAT/tax reports
- **HR & Payroll** with leave management and payslip generation
- **CRM and customer management**
- **Email, SMS, and WhatsApp marketing campaigns**
- **Project management** with Kanban-style task boards
- **Helpdesk ticketing** for customer support
- **Appointment scheduling**
- **Fleet and vehicle tracking**
- **Recruitment pipeline** management
- **Attendance tracking** with clock-in/clock-out
- **A full public-facing e-commerce storefront**
- **Affiliate and referral programs**
- **API webhooks** for third-party integrations
- **Role-based staff access control**
- **Multi-branch management**

In short, ExiusCart replaces the need for a dozen different software subscriptions and gives you one clean, beautifully designed interface to run every dimension of your business.

## 9. Security, Roles & Staff Management {#security}

Security and access control are foundational to any business management platform. ExiusCart implements multiple layers of protection:

### Authentication
- **JWT-based authentication** — every API request is authenticated with a secure, short-lived JSON Web Token
- **Automatic session expiry** — inactive sessions are terminated to prevent unauthorized access
- **Automatic redirect to login** on 401 responses — if your session expires, you are safely redirected to the login page without exposing any data

### Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
Not every staff member should have access to every module. ExiusCart's role system allows shop owners and administrators to:

- Define roles with specific permission sets
- Assign roles to staff members at the time of invitation
- Restrict access to sensitive modules (Accounting, HR & Payroll, Billing) to senior staff only
- Prevent cashiers from accessing financial reports or employee salary data
- Audit user activity through system logs

### Data Ownership
Each shop's data is fully isolated from other shops on the platform. Every database query is scoped to the authenticated user's `shop_id`, ensuring complete data separation between customers. You cannot access another business's data, and your data cannot be accessed by other users.

### HTTPS & Encrypted Communication
All communication between the ExiusCart frontend and backend is encrypted via HTTPS. API keys, webhook secrets, and authentication tokens are never exposed in plain text in the application interface.

## 10. API, Webhooks & Integrations {#api-webhooks}

ExiusCart is built API-first. Every piece of functionality available in the dashboard is exposed through a clean, documented REST API. This means developers can build custom integrations, automate workflows, and connect ExiusCart to any third-party system.

### Webhooks
Webhooks allow ExiusCart to push real-time notifications to your external systems when specific events occur. The Webhooks module lets you:

- **Register webhook endpoints** — provide your server's URL to receive event notifications
- **Set a webhook secret** — a shared secret used to verify that webhook payloads are genuinely from ExiusCart
- **Select events to subscribe to** — choose exactly which events trigger notifications:
  - Order created
  - Order status updated
  - Product stock below threshold
  - Payment received
  - And more
- **Test webhooks** — trigger a test payload to verify your endpoint is receiving correctly
- **View webhook delivery logs** — see every webhook delivery attempt, response code, and timestamp for debugging
- **Enable/disable webhooks** without deleting the configuration

### Common Webhook Use Cases
- Trigger a WhatsApp notification to your warehouse manager when a new order is placed
- Update your ERP or accounting software when an order is completed
- Send a Slack notification when stock falls below minimum levels
- Fire off an automated SMS to a customer when their order status changes
- Connect to custom loyalty or rewards platforms

### REST API
The ExiusCart REST API follows standard RESTful conventions:
- JSON request and response bodies

## 13. Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

**Q: Can I use ExiusCart without any technical knowledge?**
A: Absolutely. ExiusCart is built for business owners, not developers. The interface is intuitive and every module is designed to be self-explanatory. Setup takes hours, not weeks.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Your shop data is fully isolated from all other ExiusCart customers. JWT-based authentication ensures only authorized users can access your account.

**Q: Can I change my plan at any time?**
A: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Billing module. Plan changes take effect immediately.

**Q: What happens if I need more staff accounts than my plan includes?**
A: You can add additional staff seat add-ons from the Billing module. Pricing is per seat per month (AED 59, LKR 4,900, or $16 depending on your currency). There is no limit on how many additional seats you can purchase.

**Q: Does ExiusCart work for service businesses that do not sell physical products?**
A: Yes. The Appointments, Projects, Helpdesk, and HR modules are specifically designed for service businesses. You can use ExiusCart without ever using the Inventory or POS modules.

**Q: Can I use ExiusCart in Arabic?**
A: ExiusCart includes the Cairo typeface for full Arabic text support. RTL layout support is built into the platform's design system.

**Q: What is DKC and when will crypto payments be available?**
A: DKC is a cryptocurrency planned for integration with ExiusCart's subscription billing. The goal is to make ExiusCart accessible to users who prefer or can only transact in crypto. The launch date will be announced through official ExiusCart channels. Users can opt into DKC payment when it launches — it will be available as an alternative to card payments, not a replacement.

**Q: Does ExiusCart work offline?**
A: ExiusCart is a cloud-based platform and requires an internet connection for full functionality. However, the application is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on your device for a native app-like experience.

**Q: Can I have multiple shops under one account?**
A: The multi-branch feature allows you to manage multiple locations under one account. Each branch operates with its own data context while remaining under your central administration.

**Q: What payment methods does ExiusCart accept for subscriptions?**
A: Currently, ExiusCart accepts Visa and Mastercard credit/debit cards. The card must be issued in your region (UAE bank card for AED billing, Sri Lankan bank card for LKR billing, international card for USD billing). DKC crypto payments are coming soon.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: ExiusCart offers a trial period for new registrations. No credit card is required to sign up and explore the platform.

**Q: Can ExiusCart generate VAT-compliant invoices for the UAE?**
A: Yes. ExiusCart fully supports UAE VAT (5%) with proper invoice formatting, VAT amount breakdown, and quarterly VAT reports in a format aligned with UAE Federal Tax Authority requirements.
